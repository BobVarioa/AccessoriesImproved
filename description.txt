Combined Accessories Improved is a mod that adds some new Accessories and combined versions of existing ones

C.A.I Includes 23 new Accessories, 21 of those being new / combined accessories!

Including :

Final upgrades for all classes: Solar Knuckle Dusters, Stardust Dragonling, Vortex Bracers, Emblem of Mana

Two new upgrades of the Ankh Shield : Holy Ankh Shield, Shield of Anubis

And some upgrades for essential late game Accessories.

NOTE : All sprites are not final! They are all placeholder sprites (Except one it should be obvious which).
